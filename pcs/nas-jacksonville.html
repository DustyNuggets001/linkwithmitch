VA Loan & PCS Tips for NAS Jacksonville

  • Second-Tier Entitlement: Keep your current VA-financed home and buy at your new duty station—common for Jacksonville PCS moves
  • Own Two VA Homes Simultaneously: Use remaining VA entitlement if you have sufficient eligibility and the loan amount fits within county limits
  • Remote Closing Coordination: I work with title companies nationwide to coordinate closings around your PCS timeline
  • No Down Payment Required: Eligible service members can purchase with 0% down using VA benefits
  • Licensed in All 50 States: Whether you're coming from or going to another state, I can help with both ends of your PCS move
  • Florida Property Insurance: I can connect you with local insurance agents familiar with Florida homeowners insurance requirements
