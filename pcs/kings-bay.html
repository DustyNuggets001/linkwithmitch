Base Overview

  • Location: St. Marys, Georgia (Camden County)
  • Mission: East Coast home port for Ohio-class ballistic missile submarines and guided missile submarines
  • Population: Approximately 9,000 active duty, reserve, DoD civilians, and contractors
  • Major Commands: Submarine Group 10, Strategic Weapons Facility Atlantic
  • On-Base Housing: Available through Lendlease (formerly Balfour Beatty Communities)
  • Base Website: CNIC Kings Bay

Where to Live Off-Base

Popular neighborhoods near Kings Bay offer diverse options from waterfront homes to suburban family communities.

St. Marys

Commute: 5-15 minutes to base

Vibe: Historic downtown, waterfront access, family-friendly, walkable riverfront district

Typical Prices: $200K-$350K | Rentals: $1,400-$2,200/month

Schools: Camden County School District (St. Marys Elementary, Middle, High)

Kingsland

Commute: 15-20 minutes to base

Vibe: Newer developments, shopping centers, suburban feel, growing rapidly

Typical Prices: $220K-$380K | Rentals: $1,500-$2,400/month

Schools: Camden County schools; convenient to multiple schools

Woodbine

Commute: 20-25 minutes to base

Vibe: Rural, more land, quieter, potential for USDA loans

Typical Prices: $180K-$300K | Rentals: $1,200-$1,900/month

Schools: Camden County schools; county seat location

Fernandina Beach, FL

Commute: 30-40 minutes to base (cross state line)

Vibe: Beach town, historic downtown, tourism, higher cost of living

Typical Prices: $350K-$600K+ | Rentals: $2,000-$3,500/month

Schools: Nassau County Schools (FL) - highly rated

Brunswick

Commute: 35-45 minutes to base

Vibe: Larger city, more amenities, hospital, college, coastal access

Typical Prices: $180K-$400K | Rentals: $1,300-$2,200/month

Schools: Glynn County Schools; more options including private schools

Commute & Transportation

Traffic Patterns

Most common routes to base are via GA-40 and Spur 40. Traffic is generally light except during shift changes (0630-0730, 1530-1630).

Gate Information

  • Main Gate: GA-40/Spur 40 - Primary access, can have backups during morning rush
  • Back Gate: Charlie Smith Sr. Highway - Often faster during peak times

Tips

  • No tolls between local communities and base
  • GA-40 is the main artery; well-maintained and straightforward
  • If living in Fernandina Beach (FL), expect bridge traffic on A1A
  • Consider gate proximity when choosing neighborhoods

Schools & Childcare

Camden County School District (Georgia)

All off-base housing in St. Marys, Kingsland, and Woodbine falls under Camden County Schools.

  • Elementary: Crooked River, David L. Rainer, Kingsland, Mamie Lou Gross, Mary Lee Clark, Matilda Harris, St. Marys, Sugarmill, Woodbine
  • Middle: Camden County Middle School, St. Marys Middle School
  • High: Camden County High School
  • Alternative: Camden Success Academy
  • Website: Camden County Schools

On-Base Childcare

  • Kings Bay CDC: Child Development Center on base for infants through age 5
  • Youth Programs: School-age care, summer camps, sports
  • Registration: Military Child Care (waitlists common; register early)

Private & Daycare Options

Multiple private daycares and preschools in St. Marys and Kingsland. Average cost: $150-$250/week depending on age and program.

VA & PCS Loan Tips for Kings Bay

VA Loan Advantages

  • 0% Down Payment: Purchase with no money down
  • No Monthly MI: Unlike FHA or conventional loans under 20% down
  • Competitive Rates: Often lower than conventional financing
  • Flexible Credit: I work with all credit levels

Second-Tier Entitlement

Many submariners get orders back to Kings Bay multiple times. Depending on your situation, second-tier entitlement may allow you to keep your current home while purchasing at your new duty station. Let's discuss whether this option is available for you.

USDA Loans in Woodbine & Rural Areas

Much of Camden County qualifies for USDA loans with 0% down for eligible rural properties. Great alternative to VA if you want to preserve VA entitlement or don't have VA eligibility yet.

Closing Cost Strategy

  • Seller Credits: In this market, asking for 3-6% seller credits toward closing costs is common
  • Rate Buydowns: Use seller credits to buy down your rate or cover VA funding fee
  • Timing: Start pre-qualification as soon as you get orders; aim to close within 30-45 days of arrival
