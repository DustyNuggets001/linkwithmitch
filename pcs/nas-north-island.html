VA Loan & PCS Tips for NAS North Island

  • San Diego Market: High-cost area with VA Jumbo options available—let's maximize your buying power
  • Second-Tier Entitlement: May allow keeping your current VA-financed home while buying at your new duty station—let's discuss your situation
  • Own Two VA Homes Simultaneously: Potentially available depending on your entitlement and loan amounts—let's review your options
  • Remote Closing Coordination: I work with title companies nationwide to coordinate closings around your PCS timeline
  • No Down Payment Required: Eligible service members can purchase with 0% down using VA benefits
  • Licensed in All 50 States: Whether you're coming from or going to another state, I can help with both ends of your PCS move
