PCS to Kings Bay? How to Use Your VA Loan and Plan Your Move

A clear, compliant guide for military families relocating to Naval Submarine Base Kings Bay.

Orders to Kings Bay can come fast. Here’s a step-by-step way to align your PCS timeline with your VA loan options—without promises or rate talk.

Step 1 – Confirm Your Entitlement and Occupancy Plan

Review your VA entitlement and how you plan to occupy the home. If you still own a VA-financed home, we may need to look at remaining entitlement and county limits. Occupancy expectations apply; I’ll walk you through typical VA guidelines (no legal or tax advice).

Step 2 – Check BAH and Local Housing Costs

Use the official DoD calculator to see BAH for Kings Bay by rank and dependent status. Compare rent vs. own scenarios based on your goals—no payment guarantees, just clarity.

Check BAH at Kings Bay

Step 3 – Understand Your Loan Options

  • VA: No down payment for eligible buyers; funding fee may apply; program and eligibility vary.
  • Conventional/FHA/USDA: May fit certain buyers or properties. USDA can be strong in eligible areas of Camden County; FHA offers low down payment; conventional may suit stronger credit profiles.

Program availability and guidelines vary; we’ll review your situation to see what fits.

Step 4 – Pick Areas to Live

Popular nearby areas include Kingsland, St. Marys, and Woodbine. Consider commute times to base, schools, and amenities. I can share local context and connect you with community resources.

Step 5 – Documents and Timeline Prep

Common items: COE, LES, PCS orders, ID, pay history, assets, housing history. Timelines depend on volume and completeness; we can often start the pre-qualification process the same day once required info is received.

Common PCS & VA Questions

  • Can I have two VA loans at once? Sometimes, if remaining entitlement and county limits support it. We’ll calculate your scenario together.
  • Do I need to sell before I buy? Not always; depends on entitlement, DTI, and local limits.
  • How fast can I get a pre-qualification? Often same day once required info is received, depending on volume and completeness.
