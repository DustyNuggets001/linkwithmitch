PCS to Kings Bay? How to Use Your VA Loan and Plan Your Move

A clear, compliant guide for military families relocating to Naval Submarine Base Kings Bay.

PCSing to Kings Bay? I can help you navigate your VA loan and entitlement so you don't have to sort it out alone. Here's how we'll approach it together:

Review Your VA Entitlement and Occupancy Plan

We'll look at your Certificate of Eligibility (COE) and any existing VA loans to figure out your remaining entitlement—especially if you still own a VA-financed home from a previous duty station. I'll help you understand if you can use remaining entitlement to buy at Kings Bay while keeping your current property as a rental, or if you'll need to sell first. We'll also walk through Camden County loan limits, occupancy requirements, and what makes sense for your PCS timeline and goals.

Check BAH for Kings Bay

Look up BAH by rank and dependent status to understand what rent vs. own looks like for your situation. This helps you set realistic expectations for housing costs in the area.

Check BAH at Kings Bay

Explore Your Loan Options

  • VA: No down payment for eligible buyers; funding fee may apply; program and eligibility vary.
  • USDA: Available in eligible areas of Camden County for qualifying buyers.
  • FHA/Conventional: May fit certain buyers or properties when VA or USDA don't align.

Program availability and guidelines vary; we'll review your situation to see what fits.

Pick Areas to Live

Nearby areas include Kingsland, St. Marys, Woodbine, Brunswick, Yulee, and Jacksonville. Consider commute times to base, schools, and amenities. I can share local context and connect you with community resources.

Gather Your Documents

Have LES, PCS orders, and pay history? Great! If you don't have your COE yet, I'll help you obtain it quickly. I'll walk you through what's needed and can assist with the VA's online system. Timelines depend on having docs available; we can often start same day once required info is received, depending on volume and completeness.

Common PCS & VA Questions

  • Can I have two VA loans at once? Sometimes, if remaining entitlement and county limits support it. This is called second-tier entitlement, and it's common during PCS moves when selling your previous home isn't practical. I'll calculate your specific scenario and help you decide if carrying two VA loans makes sense for your goals.
  • Do I need to sell before I buy? Not always; depends on entitlement, DTI, and local limits. If you have sufficient remaining entitlement and your income supports carrying two mortgages (or you plan to rent out your current home), you may be able to buy at Kings Bay without selling first.
  • How do you help with the entitlement calculation? I pull your COE, review your existing loan details, and calculate how much entitlement you have available. Then I compare that to Camden County loan limits and your target purchase price to determine your options. I explain the math clearly so you understand your buying power before house hunting.
  • How fast can I get a pre-qualification? Often same day once required info is received, depending on volume and completeness. For PCS moves, I prioritize getting you pre-qualified quickly so you can make competitive offers as soon as you arrive.
