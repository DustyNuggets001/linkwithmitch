VA Loans

No down payment, no monthly mortgage insurance for eligible service members, veterans, and surviving spouses.

Key Benefits

  • 0% Down Payment: Purchase with no money down
  • No Monthly MI: Unlike FHA or conventional with less than 20% down
  • Competitive Rates: Often lower than conventional financing
  • Flexible Credit Guidelines: Work with clients across credit spectrums
  • Second-Tier Entitlement: Own two VA-financed homes simultaneously in certain scenarios

Who Qualifies?

Active-duty service members, veterans, National Guard, Reservists (with qualifying service), and surviving spouses of those who died in service or from service-connected disabilities.

VA IRRRL (Streamline Refinance)

Interest Rate Reduction Refinance Loan - streamlined refinance for existing VA loans with minimal documentation.

Benefits

  • Lower your interest rate and monthly payment
  • Minimal documentation required
  • No appraisal in most cases
  • Refinance up to 100% of current loan balance plus funding fee
  • Can include energy-efficient improvements

Requirements: Must currently have a VA loan, be current on payments, and certify you previously occupied the property.

FHA Loans

Federal Housing Administration loans with as little as 3.5% down and flexible credit guidelines.

Key Features

  • Low Down Payment: As low as 3.5% with qualifying credit
  • Flexible Credit: More lenient credit requirements than conventional
  • Gift Funds Allowed: Down payment can be gifted from family
  • Assumable: Loan can be transferred to future buyer

Considerations

  • Upfront mortgage insurance premium (can be financed)
  • Monthly mortgage insurance required
  • Property must meet FHA standards

Conventional Loans

Traditional financing with as little as 3% down for qualified first-time buyers, 5% for repeat buyers.

Advantages

  • Competitive Rates: Often best rates for strong credit
  • PMI Can Be Removed: Once you reach 20% equity
  • Higher Loan Limits: Up to $766,550 in most areas (2024)
  • Flexible Property Types: Primary, second homes, investment properties

First-Time Buyer Programs

As low as 3% down for first-time buyers. Ask about HomeReady and Home Possible programs with expanded income and credit flexibility.

Jumbo Loans

Financing for higher-priced homes that exceed conventional conforming limits.

Features

  • Loan amounts above $766,550 (varies by county)
  • Competitive rates for qualified borrowers
  • Purchase or refinance
  • Primary, second homes, and investment properties

Requirements

Typically requires stronger credit (680+), larger down payment (10-20%), and lower debt-to-income ratios. VA Jumbo loans available for eligible service members and veterans.
