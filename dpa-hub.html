Down Payment Assistance

Get Help with Your Down Payment

Georgia Dream, VA Peach Select, USDA, FHLBank & Essex Programs

Quick Summary

Down payment assistance (DPA) programs help qualified buyers cover the upfront costs of purchasing a home. Many programs offer grants or low-interest loans that reduce or eliminate the need for a large cash down payment.

Available in Camden County, Kingsland, St. Marys, Woodbine, North Florida & South Georgia:

  • Georgia Dream: Down payment and closing cost grants for first-time buyers
  • VA + VA Peach Select: Additional assistance for eligible military buyers
  • USDA 0% Down: No down payment required in eligible Camden County areas
  • FHLBank Programs: Flexible assistance for income-qualified buyers
  • Essex Programs: Options for experienced buyers and repeat purchasers

Many programs can be combined (e.g., Georgia Dream + FHA, VA + VA Peach Select, USDA + local grants).

What Is Down Payment Assistance?

Down payment assistance (DPA) programs provide financial help to qualified homebuyers, making homeownership more accessible by reducing the cash needed upfront.

How DPA Works

DPA programs typically offer:

  • Grants: Free money that does not need to be repaid (most common)
  • Forgivable Loans: Loans forgiven after you live in the home for a set period (e.g., 5-10 years)
  • Low-Interest Loans: Second mortgages with low or deferred interest
  • Matched Savings: Programs that match your savings contributions

What DPA Can Cover

  • Down payment (typically 3-5% of purchase price)
  • Closing costs (lender fees, title, appraisal, inspections)
  • Prepaid items (property taxes, insurance, HOA dues)
  • Combination of down payment and closing costs

Benefit: Many buyers can purchase a home with little to no money out of pocket when combining DPA with low- or zero-down loan programs like VA, USDA, FHA, or conventional 97% LTV loans.

Georgia Dream

Georgia's premier down payment assistance program, offering grants up to $12,500 for qualified first-time homebuyers.

Program Highlights

  • Grant Amount: Up to $12,500 (does not need to be repaid)
  • Use: Down payment and/or closing costs
  • Loan Types: Can be combined with FHA, conventional, USDA, or VA loans
  • First-Time Buyer: Generally required (some exceptions for military, veterans, and targeted areas)
  • Income Limits: Vary by county and household size
  • Purchase Price Limits: Caps vary by location
  • Homebuyer Education: Required online course (usually 6-8 hours)

Who Qualifies?

  • First-time homebuyer (or meet exception criteria)
  • Purchasing in Georgia (including Camden County, Kingsland, St. Marys, Woodbine)
  • Meet income and purchase price limits for your county
  • Complete homebuyer education course
  • Meet credit and debt-to-income requirements
  • Occupy home as primary residence

Common Combinations:

  • Georgia Dream + FHA 3.5% down
  • Georgia Dream + Conventional 97% LTV (3% down)
  • Georgia Dream + USDA 0% down (covers closing costs)

VA + VA Peach Select

Exclusive program for Georgia military buyers that provides additional down payment or closing cost assistance on top of VA loan benefits.

Program Highlights

  • Grant Amount: Varies; designed to supplement VA loans
  • Eligibility: Active duty, veterans, National Guard, Reservists purchasing in Georgia
  • Use: Closing costs, prepaid items, or down payment
  • Combines With VA Loan: Already 0% down, so DPA covers out-of-pocket costs
  • First-Time Buyer Not Required: Available to repeat buyers

Why VA Buyers Use VA Peach Select

Even though VA loans require no down payment, buyers still face:

  • VA funding fee (unless exempt)
  • Closing costs (lender fees, title, appraisal)
  • Prepaid property taxes and insurance
  • HOA transfer fees (if applicable)

VA Peach Select helps cover these costs, allowing military buyers to purchase with minimal cash out of pocket.

Perfect For

  • PCS military families moving to Camden County, Kingsland, St. Marys
  • Veterans purchasing near Naval Submarine Base Kings Bay
  • Service members buying first or second homes in Georgia
  • Those who want to preserve savings for moving expenses and emergencies

FHLBank Programs

Federal Home Loan Bank (FHLBank) programs provide flexible down payment and closing cost assistance for income-qualified buyers.

Program Highlights

  • Grant Amounts: Typically $5,000-$7,500 (varies by program availability)
  • Use: Down payment and/or closing costs
  • Loan Types: FHA, conventional, VA, USDA
  • Income Limits: Generally 80% or less of area median income (AMI)
  • First-Time Buyer Preference: But not always required
  • Homebuyer Education: May be required

Who Qualifies?

  • Income at or below program limits (moderate to low income)
  • Purchasing a primary residence
  • Meet lender credit and underwriting standards
  • Complete homebuyer education (if required)

Availability

FHLBank programs are subject to funding availability and typically offered in limited annual rounds. Contact me early to check current availability and reserve funds.

Essex Programs

Essex Mortgage offers flexible down payment assistance programs for both first-time and experienced buyers.

Program Highlights

  • Available to Repeat Buyers: Not limited to first-time homebuyers
  • Loan Types: FHA, conventional, VA
  • Grant or Forgivable Loan: Structure varies by program
  • Income Limits: Vary by county and program
  • Use: Down payment and/or closing costs

Who Benefits?

  • Experienced buyers who no longer qualify as "first-time"
  • Military families using VA loans (can stack with VA benefits)
  • Buyers seeking alternative DPA options
  • Those who need flexibility in loan type or income qualification

Note: Essex program availability and terms vary. I'll review current options and determine if you qualify based on your specific situation.

Who Qualifies for Down Payment Assistance?

Eligibility varies by program, but common factors include:

Common Eligibility Factors

Income Limits

Most programs require household income at or below a percentage of area median income (AMI). Limits vary by county, household size, and program.

Loan Type

DPA can be combined with FHA, VA, USDA, conventional loans. Some programs have restrictions or preferences.

Location

Property must be in an eligible area (e.g., Georgia for Georgia Dream, USDA-eligible zones for USDA, etc.).

First-Time Buyer Status

Many programs require first-time homebuyer status. Exceptions often apply for military, veterans, and buyers in targeted areas.

Credit & DTI

Must meet lender credit score and debt-to-income (DTI) requirements (typically 620+ credit, DTI under 43-50%).

Homebuyer Education

Many programs require completion of an approved homebuyer education course (usually online, 6-8 hours).

Primary Residence

Home must be your primary residence (no investment properties or second homes).

Military Status

Some programs (VA Peach Select) are exclusive to active duty, veterans, National Guard, and Reservists.

Not sure if you qualify? I'll review your income, location, loan type, and eligibility for all available DPA programs to find the best fit.

Can DPA Programs Be Combined?

Yes! Many down payment assistance programs can be stacked with low- or zero-down loan programs to maximize your buying power.

Common Combinations

Georgia Dream + FHA

FHA: 3.5% down
Georgia Dream: Up to $12,500 grant covers down payment + closing costs
Result: Minimal cash out of pocket

VA + VA Peach Select

VA Loan: 0% down
VA Peach Select: Grant covers closing costs & VA funding fee
Result: True $0 out of pocket for eligible military buyers

USDA + Georgia Dream

USDA: 0% down
Georgia Dream: Grant covers closing costs
Result: 100% financing + $0 closing costs

Conventional 97% + FHLBank

Conventional: 3% down
FHLBank: $5,000-$7,500 grant
Result: Low down payment + grant assistance

Restrictions

  • Some programs cannot be combined (check program rules)
  • Total assistance may be capped as a percentage of purchase price or loan amount
  • Lender and investor overlays may apply
  • Underwriting approval required for all combinations

I'll identify the best combination for your situation and ensure all programs are properly coordinated.
