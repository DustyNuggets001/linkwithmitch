What Is Down Payment Assistance?

Down Payment Assistance (DPA) includes grants, forgivable loans, deferred-payment second mortgages, or interest-rate subsidies designed to help eligible buyers reduce upfront costs.

These programs may help with:

  • Down payment
  • Closing costs
  • Prepaids
  • Discounted interest rates (e.g., VA Peach Select)

Each program has different income limits, loan type requirements, and occupancy rules â€” I help you navigate them and determine your eligibility.

Programs I Offer or Help You Access

1. Georgia Dream (Georgia Department of Community Affairs)

A popular program for Georgia homebuyers.

Depending on eligibility, Georgia Dream may offer:

  • Down payment or closing cost assistance
  • Additional support for first-time buyers, protective service workers, or eligible veterans
  • Assistance paired with FHA, VA, USDA, or conventional loans

Program availability and funding vary; subject to change. Contact me to confirm current eligibility and availability.

2. VA Loan Benefits + VA Peach Select (For Eligible Military Buyers)

For eligible VA borrowers in Georgia, VA Peach Select may offer:

  • A subsidized interest rate
  • Additional potential savings for qualified service members and veterans

I help you determine whether you qualify and how it can be leveraged with your VA loan.

Program availability and funding vary; subject to change. Contact me to confirm current eligibility and availability.

3. USDA 0% Down Financing (Camden County & Surrounding Areas)

Much of Camden County is USDA-eligible, allowing:

  • 0% down payment for qualified buyers
  • Flexible credit guidelines
  • Low mortgage insurance (PMI equivalent)
  • Attractive rates compared to many loan programs

This is one of the strongest options for first-time or budget-conscious buyers.

USDA eligibility based on property location and household income limits; subject to change. Contact me to verify property and borrower eligibility.

5. Essex Programs (For Both First-Time and Experienced Buyers)

Not all DPA is only for first-time buyers. Through Essex, both first-time and experienced buyers may be eligible for:

  • Down payment assistance
  • Reduced cash-to-close options
  • Targeted programs based on income, location, or loan type

These options are situation-specific, and we review eligibility together.

Who Qualifies for Down Payment Assistance?

Eligibility varies by program, but factors may include:

First-Time or Repeat Buyer Status

Some programs require first-time buyer status; others are available to experienced buyers.

Household Income

Most programs have income limits based on area median income (AMI).

Debt-to-Income Ratio

Your monthly debt obligations compared to gross income must meet program guidelines.

Credit Profile

Minimum credit score requirements vary by program and loan type.

Loan Type

VA, FHA, USDA, or Conventional â€” different programs pair with different loan types.

Property Location

Eligibility may depend on whether the property is in an eligible area (e.g., USDA zones).

Community Partner Roles

Some programs (e.g., FHLBank Community Partner, Georgia Dream PEN) offer assistance to eligible educators, healthcare professionals, and first responders.

Military Service or Veteran Status

Some programs are exclusive to active duty, veterans, National Guard, and Reservists.

Primary Residence

Most DPA programs require you to occupy the home as your primary residence.

I help you determine exactly which programs fit your scenario.
